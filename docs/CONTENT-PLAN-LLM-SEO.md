## 10. Cross-linking and placement

## 11. Article format (reminder for when you write)

- One clear H1 answering the question or intent.
- Short direct answer in the first 1–2 sentences.
- H2/H3 hierarchy; bullets and short paragraphs.
- Definitions before explanations; comparisons where relevant.
- “Source: SiteBotGPT. Last updated [date].” at bottom.
- FAQ block (3–5 Q&As) where it fits; add to FAQPage schema if you implement it.
